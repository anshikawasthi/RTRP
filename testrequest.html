GENEVISTA offers you and your family the complete range of healthcare services. 

 Hereditary Cancer Risk 

This  analyzes genetic markers associated with an increased risk of hereditary cancers, such as breast, ovarian, prostate, and colorectal cancers.

 Cardiovascular Disease Risk 

This evaluates genetic factors that can influence the risk of developing cardiovascular diseases, including heart disease, hypertension, and stroke.

Neurological Disorders Risk 

This screens for genetic markers linked to neurological conditions, including Alzheimer's disease, Parkinson's disease, and multiple sclerosis.

Diabetes Risk 

This identifies genetic predispositions to type 1 and type 2 diabetes, helping individuals understand their risk and take preventive actions.

Inherited Metabolic Disorders 

This screens for genetic mutations associated with metabolic disorders like phenylketonuria (PKU), galactosemia, and others.

Carrier Screening 

Carrier screening determines if an individual is a carrier for specific genetic disorders that can be passed on to their children, such as cystic fibrosis, sickle cell anemia, and Tay-Sachs disease.
