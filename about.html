about

Welcome to GENEVISTA , where we empower you with insights into your genetic makeup. At GENEVISTA , we believe that understanding your DNA can unlock a wealth of information about your health, ancestry, and personal traits. Our mission is to make genetic testing accessible, informative, and actionable for everyone.

OUR COMMITMENT

ACCURACY: We utilize state-of-the-art technology and partner with accredited laboratories to ensure the highest standards of accuracy and reliability in our DNA tests.
PRIVACY: Your privacy and security are our top priorities. We adhere to strict protocols and comply with all relevant data protection regulations to safeguard your genetic information.
EDUCATION: We strive to make genetic science understandable and applicable to everyday life. Our reports are designed to provide clear insights and actionable recommendations based on your genetic profile.
SUPPORT: Our team of genetic counselors and customer support specialists are here to guide you through every step of your DNA testing journey. We're committed to ensuring that you have the support you need, from test selection to understanding your results.

WHY CHOOSE US?

COMPREHENSIVE TESTS: We offer a range of DNA tests tailored to meet your specific needs, whether you're curious about your ancestry, interested in personalized health insights, or seeking to understand inherited traits.
EASY PROCESS: Getting started is simple. Order your test kit online, collect your sample from the comfort of your home, and send it back to us for analysis. Receive your detailed report securely online.
SCIENTIFIC EXCELLENCE: Our tests are developed in collaboration with leading geneticists and scientists. We're dedicated to advancing genetic research and translating scientific discoveries into practical applications for our customers.

REPORT PROCESS

As part of our methodology, our CLIA-certified lab extracts DNA from cells in your saliva sample. Then the lab processes the DNA on a genotyping chip that reads hundreds of thousands of variants in your genome.
Your genetic data is analyzed, and we generate your personalized reports based on well-established scientific and medical research.
